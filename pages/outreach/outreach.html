The Brambleton Lyme 5K

The Brambleton Lyme 5K is a run held annually by Loudoun Lyme in order to support Capital Lyme. The run is used to raise money and awareness for Lyme Disease. Our team set up a booth at the run and we talked to people there to spread awareness about our solution to Lyme with Synthetic Biology.
